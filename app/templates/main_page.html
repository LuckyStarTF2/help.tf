Because we love tf2, love to help and meet new people.

Mentors are experienced TF2 players who want to share their experience and help new players to get into TF2. If you are one of them, you can also become a mentor.

There are two options. First, you can go to the mentors page and find somebody who looks appealing to you, depending on your needs. Then you message them on Discord and if they are available, add them on Steam. The second option is to just join our discord server and ask for help in the #help-requests channel. Specify your location (like Europe or North America) and language so it will be easier for you to communicate with a mentor.
